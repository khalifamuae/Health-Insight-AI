## Full Description (English)

Track your health with AI precision. BioTrack AI analyzes your lab results using advanced artificial intelligence and provides personalized nutrition plans based on clinical evidence.

KEY FEATURES:

- AI Lab Analysis: Upload your PDF lab results and get instant AI-powered analysis of 50 different medical tests
- Personalized Diet Plans: Evidence-based nutrition plans tailored to your lab results, body metrics, and health goals
- Test Tracking: Monitor 50 medical tests including vitamins, minerals, hormones, organ functions, lipids, and blood markers
- Smart Comparison: Compare old vs new test results side-by-side with trend indicators
- Color-Coded Results: Instantly see what needs attention - green for normal, red for abnormal values
- Recheck Reminders: Never miss a follow-up test with automated reminders
- Clinical Nutrition: Professor-level diet plans with bioavailability optimization and scientific references
- Bilingual Support: Full Arabic and English interface

50 MEDICAL TESTS TRACKED:
Vitamins (D, B12, Folate, etc.), Minerals (Iron, Calcium, Zinc, etc.), Hormones (TSH, Testosterone, Cortisol, etc.), Organ Functions (Liver, Kidney), Lipid Panel, Blood Count, Coagulation, and more.
